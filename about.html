Purpose
I didn't choose to be an artist, but I did chose to be an illustrator. I chose illustration to learn how to bring creative value to ideals through visual communication. My desired method for this is to use simplified allegory/ metaphors to visually communicate otherwise complex ideas. Editorial Illustration is my passion but my art takes many forms, If you have a creative project in mind please contact me.
Process
My process always has handmade roots, but then grows to become heavily digital. The work is then run through a printing process, Ussually using one of the many vintage printers that fill my studio. That is how I get a grainy vintage printed look that I utilize so much. I am always striving for a minimal but meaningful visual style. A style that can speak to your audience in a clear, effective, but also quick fashion.

Commissions and Reuse
The price of editorial illustrations are dependent on the DISTRIBUTION of your publication, SIZE of the artwork and the LENGTH OF TIME the piece is used. All other projects are priced according to the project details. Beyond commissions all other work in my portfolio is available for reuse. Contact me if you would like to re-licence an already created piece of art. Price for reuse depends on these variables.
